# Frasberg AI Public FAQ

## What is Frasberg AI?

Frasberg AI is a governed intelligence company focused on structured, constitutional, and domain-aware AI systems for developers, enterprises, and global organizations.

## What is Luchii?

Luchii is the core codex model powering the Frasberg AI ecosystem. It is positioned as the central reasoning and interpretation layer across Frasberg AI domains.

## What does “governed intelligence” mean?

Governed intelligence means model behavior is shaped by constitutional rules, safety policies, domain scaffolds, and operational constraints rather than unconstrained generation alone.

## What domains does Frasberg AI organize around?

Frasberg AI currently organizes intelligence into three governed domains:

- Species Codex
- Civilizations Atlas
- Chronicles Library

## What makes Luchii different?

Luchii is designed around:

- Constitutional alignment
- Domain-aware reasoning
- Tool-call orchestration
- Structured output behavior
- Multi-region deployment principles

## Is this repository a production runtime?

No. This repository documents the Luchii codex, governance materials, and public-facing scaffolds. It does not claim to be a production runtime service.

## What APIs are described for Luchii?

Public-facing materials in this repository describe the following endpoints:

- `/chat/completions`
- `/completions`
- `/embeddings`
- `/tools/call`

## What is the Governance Constitution?

The Frasberg AI Governance Constitution defines the principles, rules, responsibilities, and safeguards that govern Frasberg AI systems and contributors.

## What is the Safety Charter?

The Safety Charter describes the public safety commitments and operational guardrails that shape Frasberg AI model behavior and governance.

## How does governance affect model behavior?

Governance affects model behavior through constitutional alignment, policy enforcement, safety filtering, redaction controls, and audit-oriented operational practices.

## What does multi-region deployment mean in this context?

Multi-region deployment refers to Frasberg AI’s architectural framing for region-aware routing, compliance considerations, reliability planning, and geographically distributed operations.

## Who is Frasberg AI built for?

Frasberg AI is designed for:

- Enterprises
- Developers
- Global organizations
- Partners that need structured and predictable AI behavior

## Is Luchii open source?

This repository contains public documentation and scaffolds. Licensing for Luchii itself is described through the Frasberg AI Enterprise License materials in this repository.
