## 4. ARCHITECTURE DEEP DIVE (12 minutes)

Let me show you how it works.

At the core is the **Autonomous Brain** — a policy engine that makes decisions based on:

- Real-time telemetry from every region
- Historical patterns and baselines
- Enterprise policies and constraints
- Safety envelope boundaries

Around that is the **Fusion Loop** — the decision-making engine that:

1. Observes the current state
2. Evaluates options against policies
3. Makes autonomous decisions
4. Executes actions
5. Monitors outcomes

But here's the thing — and this is important — the Autonomous Cloud doesn't operate in a vacuum.

It operates within **constitutional constraints**. Safety bounds. Hard limits that it cannot cross, no matter what.

And it operates under **human governance**. The ACO — Autonomous Cloud Operations — is staffed by your team. They approve policies. They audit decisions. They can override if needed. But most of the time, they're monitoring a system that just works.

The system rotates through **epochs** — 24-hour cycles where we compare the current approach against alternatives, measure the baseline, and decide whether to keep improvements or revert.

It continuously runs **evolution cycles** — testing new policies, new optimizations, new approaches in controlled conditions before deploying them globally.
