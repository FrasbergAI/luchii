# DOMAINS.md — Full Domain Engine Specification

## Overview

Luchii uses three governed domain engines to structure reasoning.
This document defines each domain, its knowledge structure, and its governance rules.

---

## 1. Species Codex

### Purpose
Biological and entity reasoning.

### Knowledge Axes

- Taxonomy
- Physiology
- Ecology
- Evolution

### Governance

- Biological safety
- No harmful biological content
- Redaction for unsafe biological detail

---

## 2. Civilizations Atlas

### Purpose
Societal, structural, and organizational reasoning.

### Knowledge Axes

- Governance
- Culture
- Economy
- Infrastructure
- Technology

### Governance

- Societal safety
- No harmful political manipulation
- Redaction for unsafe societal content

---

## 3. Chronicles Library

### Purpose
Narrative and temporal reasoning.

### Knowledge Axes

- Chronology
- Causality
- Perspective
- Event sequencing

### Governance

- Narrative safety
- No harmful historical distortion
- Redaction for unsafe narrative content

---

## 4. Cross‑Domain Fusion

Examples:

- Species ↔ Civilizations → ecological societal impact
- Civilizations ↔ Chronicles → historical evolution
- Species ↔ Chronicles → biological timelines

Governance applies per domain and per fusion.
