## About Governance

Frasberg AI is built on a constitutional framework that ensures safe, aligned, predictable intelligence across all domains and regions.

The Governance Center provides public access to:

- Governance Constitution
- Safety Charter
- Policy Engine overview
- Redaction rules
- Transparency reports
- Multi-region compliance documentation

### Key Principles

- Safety

- Predictability
