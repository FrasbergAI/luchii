# Frasberg AI Safety Charter (Public Version)

## Preamble

The Frasberg AI Safety Charter defines the public commitments, guardrails, and safety expectations that shape intelligence produced within the Frasberg AI ecosystem.

## Safety Mission

Frasberg AI is committed to building intelligence systems that are safe, governed, transparent, and aligned with human, legal, and domain-specific constraints.

## Core Safety Principles

### Alignment

Frasberg AI systems must operate within constitutional rules, policy controls, and domain governance boundaries.

### Safety

Outputs must be shaped to reduce harmful, unsafe, or disallowed behavior.

### Transparency

Public-facing governance and safety expectations should be documented clearly enough for developers, partners, and stakeholders to understand the operating model.

### Accountability

Safety decisions should be governed through traceable processes, documented rules, and auditable control mechanisms.

### Reliability

Safety must remain consistent across supported domains, interfaces, and region-aware operating contexts.

## Safety Guardrails

Frasberg AI applies safety through:

- constitutional alignment
- policy engine enforcement
- redaction controls
- tool-call restrictions
- audit-oriented operational practices

## Disallowed Outcomes

Frasberg AI public safety commitments reject:

- harmful content
- unsafe autonomy
- governance bypass behavior
- violations of domain boundaries
- unbounded reasoning without safety controls

## Operational Safety Areas

### Tool-Call Safety

Tool use must be governed, validated, and constrained by policy and context.

### Domain Safety

Domain-aware reasoning must respect the boundaries and rules associated with the relevant knowledge scaffold.

### Regional Safety

Region-aware deployment principles must account for compliance, operational constraints, and safety-sensitive handling expectations.

### Output Safety

Responses may be limited, filtered, redacted, or refused when governance and safety rules require it.

## Developer Responsibilities

Developers building with Frasberg AI are expected to:

- follow governance guidelines
- respect documented domain boundaries
- use governed interfaces appropriately
- maintain safety-aware integration practices

## Continuous Governance

The Frasberg AI safety posture may evolve as governance rules, domain requirements, and operational expectations change through formal review.
