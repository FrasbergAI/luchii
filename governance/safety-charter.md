# Frasberg AI Safety Charter
Version 1.0 — © 2026 Frasberg AI

## Mission
Frasberg AI is committed to building safe, governed, transparent artificial intelligence systems that respect human rights, protect users, and maintain alignment across all domains.

## Core Principles
1. **Alignment**  
   All models must follow constitutional safety rules and domain-specific governance.

2. **Transparency**  
   Safety decisions, policies, and updates must be documented and publicly accessible.

3. **Governance**  
   Every domain (Species, Civilizations, Chronicles, Engine) must maintain its own constitution.

4. **Redaction & Filtering**  
   Harmful, sensitive, or disallowed content must be filtered through the Policy Engine.

5. **Tool-Call Safety**  
   Tools must be governed, logged, and restricted by domain rules.

6. **Multi-Region Safety**  
   Each region must follow local laws, data residency rules, and safety profiles.

7. **Auditability**  
   All model actions must be traceable and reviewable.

## Enforcement
Violations trigger:
- Policy engine intervention  
- Governance review  
- Potential suspension of access
