## 1. FLIA Overview

FLIA supports judges, lawyers, and clerks with:

- **Evidence integrity analysis** — contradictions, gaps, timelines
- **Law and precedent mapping** — statutes, case law, procedures
- **Integrity and fraud advisory** — non-binding risk indicators
- **Full audit and governance transparency** — every action logged

**Core Promise:** FLIA does not adjudicate, infer intent, or determine guilt.  
**Core Requirement:** All outputs require human verification.

## 3. Ethics Charter (Summary)

### Core Principles

1. **Human Accountability** — Judges & lawyers remain responsible; FLIA is advisory
2. **Transparency** — All outputs explainable and traceable
3. **Rights Protection** — Presumption of innocence, due process, right to appeal
4. **Bias Mitigation** — No protected characteristics in risk signals
5. **Due Process** — Full rights protections in UI/UX
6. **Advisory-Only** — FLIA never issues verdicts or sentencing

### Prohibited Uses

- ❌ Autonomous adjudication
- ❌ Guilt/innocence determination
- ❌ Sentencing recommendations
- ❌ Profiling based on race, gender, disability, national origin

### Oversight

- **FLIA Ethics & Governance Committee** — monthly review
- **Mandatory Audit Logging** — every action tracked
- **Incident Response** — feature suspension if risk detected
- **Quarterly Audits** — performance, bias, compliance
