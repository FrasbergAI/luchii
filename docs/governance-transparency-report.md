# Frasberg AI Public Governance Transparency Report

## Introduction

Frasberg AI is committed to transparency in how governed intelligence operates.  
This report outlines the constitutional, safety, and governance systems that shape Luchii and all Frasberg AI models.

## 1. Governance Framework

### Governance Constitution

Defines:

- Allowed behaviors
- Disallowed behaviors
- Alignment rules
- Safety principles

### Safety Charter

Ensures:

- Predictable outputs
- Harm prevention
- Domain-aware constraints

### Policy Engine

Applies dynamic rules based on:

- Region
- Domain
- Context

### Redaction Layer

Removes unsafe or disallowed content.

## 2. Auditability

### Audit Logging

Tracks:

- Region ID
- Policy enforcement
- Safety filters
- Tool-call decisions

### Transparency Reports

Published regularly to show:

- Governance updates
- Safety improvements
- Policy changes

## 3. Multi-Region Compliance

Frasberg AI supports:

- Region-specific routing
- Data residency compliance
- Localized audit logs
- Region locking for enterprises

## 4. Developer Responsibilities

Developers must:

- Follow governance guidelines
- Respect domain boundaries
- Maintain safety compliance
- Use signed commits

## 5. Continuous Improvement

Governance evolves through:

- Constitutional amendments
- Safety charter updates
- Domain steward contributions
- Enterprise feedback

## Conclusion

Frasberg AI is committed to delivering intelligence that is:

- Governed
- Safe
- Transparent
- Predictable
- Region-aware

Across every deployment environment.
