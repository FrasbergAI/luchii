# Frasberg AI Brand Narrative

## The Story

Frasberg AI was founded on a simple belief:  
Intelligence must be governed.

Not restricted — governed.  
Not limited — structured.  
Not opaque — transparent.

We build AI systems that operate within constitutional frameworks, ensuring safety, alignment, and predictable behavior across every domain.

## The Philosophy

Modern AI is powerful, but power without structure leads to chaos.  
Frasberg AI introduces a new paradigm: governed intelligence — models that reason within rules, policies, and domain scaffolds.

Luchii is the embodiment of this philosophy.

## The Architecture

Frasberg AI is built on three pillars:

### 1. The Codex (Luchii)

The central intelligence engine.

### 2. The Domains

Species, Civilizations, Chronicles — structured knowledge layers.

### 3. The Governance Constitution

Safety, alignment, and policy enforcement.

Together, they form a governed intelligence ecosystem.

## The Future

Frasberg AI is not just launching a model — we’re launching a movement.  
A future where intelligence is reliable, structured, and aligned with human values.
