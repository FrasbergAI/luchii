# Frasberg AI Global Brand Book

## 1. Brand Essence

**Frasberg AI**  
**Governed Intelligence. Structured Futures.**

Frasberg AI stands for intelligence that is constitutional, predictable, multi-domain, and globally compliant. The brand communicates clarity, structure, and authority.

## 2. Brand Pillars

### Governed Intelligence
AI built on constitutional rules, safety, and alignment.

### Structured Reasoning
Models that produce predictable, stable, domain-aware outputs.

### Multi-Domain Capability
- Species Codex
- Civilizations Atlas
- Chronicles Library

### Global Reliability
Multi-region deployment, compliance, and auditability.

### Developer Empowerment
Clean APIs, SDKs, and governance documentation.

## 3. Visual Identity

### Color System
