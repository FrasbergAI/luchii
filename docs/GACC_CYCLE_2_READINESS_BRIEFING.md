## Cycle 2 Governance Directives

### Directive 1 — Constitution Review
The governance constitution (`governance/constitution.md`) shall be reviewed and amended as needed to address Cycle 2 requirements. Any amendments require GACC ratification.

### Directive 2 — Expanded Branch Protection
All feature branches shall be subject to governance review prior to PR creation. No exceptions without GACC override authorization.

### Directive 3 — Contributor Governance
All new contributors must complete governance onboarding before repository access is granted. CODEOWNERS shall be updated to reflect any governance role changes.

### Directive 4 — Documentation Governance
All platform documentation changes shall be routed through the `docs/*` branch governance path. Unreviewed documentation changes are prohibited on `main`.

### Directive 5 — Audit Continuity
The governance audit trail established in Cycle 1 shall be maintained without interruption into Cycle 2. All Cycle 1 artifacts remain authoritative and immutable.
