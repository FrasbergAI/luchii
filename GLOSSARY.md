# GLOSSARY.md — Official Frasberg AI Terminology

**Codex**
The central intelligence engine powering Luchii.

**Governed Intelligence**
AI constrained by constitutional rules, policy engine decisions, and redaction.

**Constitution**
The foundational governance document defining allowed/disallowed behaviors.

**Policy Engine**
Dynamic rule evaluator based on region, domain, and context.

**Redaction Layer**
Removes unsafe or disallowed content.

**Domain Engine**
Structured knowledge layer (Species, Civilizations, Chronicles).

**Domain Router**
Determines which domain(s) apply to a request.

**Tool‑Call Engine**
Executes governed functions with schema validation.

**Region Router**
Routes requests to region‑specific deployments.

**Data Residency**
Requirement that logs and metadata remain in the region of inference.

**Audit Logging**
Recording governance decisions, redactions, and tool‑calls.

**Multi‑Domain Reasoning**
Combining multiple domain engines in a single inference.

**Governance Council**
Authority overseeing constitutional amendments and safety rules.

**Enterprise Overlay**
Custom governance rules applied for enterprise deployments.
