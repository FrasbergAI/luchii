# Frasberg AI Global Marketing Campaign

## Campaign Title

"Governed Intelligence. Structured Futures."

## Campaign Overview

Frasberg AI's global marketing campaign introduces Luchii and the governed-intelligence paradigm to enterprises, developers, and the broader AI community.  
The campaign spans digital, social, enterprise outreach, and global events.

## Core Message

AI is powerful.  
But power without structure leads to chaos.  
Frasberg AI delivers governed intelligence — safe, predictable, multi-domain reasoning built on constitutional alignment.

## Campaign Pillars

### 1. Governed Intelligence

Highlight the constitutional foundation of Frasberg AI.

### 2. Multi-Domain Capability

Showcase Species Codex, Civilizations Atlas, and Chronicles Library.

### 3. Enterprise Reliability

Emphasize multi-region deployment, compliance, and auditability.

### 4. Developer Empowerment

Promote APIs, SDKs, tutorials, and the Developer Portal.
