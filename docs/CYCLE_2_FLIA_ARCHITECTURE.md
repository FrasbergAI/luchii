## 1. FLIA PURPOSE & SCOPE

### Core Mission
FLIA is a **legal decision-support platform** that assists judges, lawyers, and legal teams by:
- Analyzing evidence for contradictions and gaps
- Surfacing relevant law and precedent
- Flagging potential fraud and conflicts of interest
- Maintaining full human oversight and control

### Constitutional Constraints
- **Advisory only** — FLIA never judges, sentences, or determines guilt
- **Human-in-the-loop** — all outputs require human verification before use
- **Transparent** — every FLIA interaction is logged and explainable
- **Rights-preserving** — presumption of innocence and due process maintained
- **Bias-mitigated** — regular audits for fairness and protected characteristics

## 3. CORE MODULES

### A) Evidence Analysis Engine
**Function:** Analyze evidence for contradictions, gaps, and inconsistencies

**Inputs:**
- Documents, transcripts, media metadata
- Timestamps, authors, chain-of-custody

**Outputs:**
- Evidence Integrity Report
- Key facts extracted
- Contradictions identified
- Timelines visualized
- Missing evidence flags

**Constraints:**
- Cannot determine guilt
- Cannot infer intent
- All findings require human verification

### B) Legal Knowledge Engine
**Function:** Surface relevant law and precedent

**Inputs:**
- Jurisdiction, matter type
- Issues identified by humans or FLIA

**Outputs:**
- Statutes and regulations
- Key precedents
- Procedural requirements
- Rights and protections

**Constraints:**
- All authorities must be manually verified before filing
- Model may miss niche or highly fact-specific precedent

### C) Integrity & Fraud Advisory Engine
**Function:** Flag possible fraud and conflicts of interest

**Inputs:**
- Financial records, contracts, communications, timelines
- Known fraud patterns (curated)

**Outputs:**
- Risk indicators (never guilt determinations)
- Potential conflicts flagged
- Suspicious patterns highlighted

**Constraints:**
- No use of protected characteristics
- Always advisory only
- Requires human investigation

### D) Audit & Governance Engine
**Function:** Log all FLIA interactions and enforce governance

**Outputs:**
- Immutable audit trail
- Ethics Charter compliance
- Policy enforcement logs
- Incident tracking

## 4. GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK

### A) Core Principles
- **Human accountability** — judges and lawyers remain responsible
- **Transparency** — all outputs explainable and traceable
- **Fairness** — no bias in risk signals
- **Due process** — rights preserved always
- **No adjudication** — FLIA never judges

### B) Governance Structure
- **FLIA Ethics & Governance Committee** — approves use cases, reviews incidents
- **Policy Framework** — approved uses (analysis, research, advisory); prohibited uses (predictions, sentencing)
- **Audit & Incident Response** — logs all interactions, investigates misuse

### C) Operational Safeguards
- Mandatory disclaimers on all outputs
- Confidence scoring and uncertainty flags
- Human-in-the-loop requirement
- Regular bias and performance audits
