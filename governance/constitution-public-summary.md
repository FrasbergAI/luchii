# Frasberg AI Governance Constitution (Public Summary)

## Preamble

The Frasberg AI Governance Constitution establishes the principles, rules, and safeguards that govern all intelligence produced by Frasberg AI systems.

## Core Principles

### Governed Intelligence

AI must operate within structured rules and constitutional boundaries.

### Safety

All outputs must comply with the Frasberg AI Safety Charter.

### Transparency

Developers must understand how governance affects model behavior.

### Alignment

Models must align with human values, legal requirements, and domain constraints.

## Governance Structure

- Governance Council — final authority
- Luchii Directorate — model architecture
- Infrastructure Authority — deployment & compliance
- Domain Stewards — Species, Civilizations, Chronicles

## Constitutional Rules

### Allowed Behavior

- Structured reasoning
- Domain-aware responses
- Safe tool-call execution
- Transparent governance compliance

### Disallowed Behavior

- Harmful content
- Unsafe autonomy
- Violations of domain rules
- Unbounded reasoning without governance

## Enforcement Systems

- Policy Engine
- Redaction Layer
- Safety Filters
- Audit Logging
- Region-specific compliance

## Developer Responsibilities

- Follow governance guidelines
- Use signed commits
- Respect domain boundaries
- Maintain safety compliance

## Amendments

The Constitution may evolve through formal Governance Council review.
